Goal

This list was created after one of my colleagues asked me about places he should visit during his time in Toronto. Since I live there only for one year, I thought it would be good to summarize and record this list so I come back to it later.

Food

  • Pho Tien Thanh - Must visit, pho and rice are amazing.

  • Dessert Lady Cafe & Bakery - Visit for cakes and sandwiches.

  • Bang Bang Ice Cream & Bakery - Best ice cream in the city.

  • Summer's Ice Cream - One of the best ice creams.

  • KIBO Secret Garden - Visit for nice sushi.

  • Coffee Lunar - Best London fog.

  • Umami Poke - Nice and healthy.

  • NEO COFFEE BAR - Good enough coffee.

  • Dineen Coffee Co. - Like to work here on the weekend.

  • Mizzica Gelateria & Cafe - Healthy ice cream.

  • Laissez Faire - Nice new place.

  • 135 ossington - Coffee and matcha.

  • Cafe Polonez - Amazing Polish food.

  • Barrel House Korchma - Great Ukrainian kitchen.

  • Aster's Refined Cakes - Visit for desserts.

  • ONE Restaurant - Fancy place.

  • Eataly Toronto - Supermarket with cafe inside.

  • Shisha Marquise - Still trying to find a good shisha, but so far using this.

Sport

  • United Boxing Club - My boxing club.

Visit

  • Art Gallery of Ontario - Not Sistine Chapel or Uffizi Gallery, but still good to visit.
  • Berczy Park - Nice little park.
  • Queens Park - Great park in the city center.
